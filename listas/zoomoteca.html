Building Stories is a graphic novel by Chris Ware that plays with the architecture of the book and the comics’ structural vocabulary (cases, frames…)

It is a box containing fourteen books of different formats: cloth-bound books, newspapers, broadsheets and flip books.

Por Richard McGuire // 2014 // Pantheon Books

Zoomoteca

Here is a comic by Richard McGuire that plays with frames representing actions happening in a same place but at different times.

Here started as a 6-page black and white comic, published in Raw Volume 2 #1 in 1989.

In 2014, McGuire published an expanded version, in a 300 pages color graphic novel (Pantheon Books).
