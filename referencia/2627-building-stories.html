Building Stories

Libro, publicado en papel o digital. En Listas compartidas
2012 Chris Ware / Pantheon Books

Building Stories is a graphic novel by Chris Ware that plays with the architecture of the book and the comics’ structural vocabulary (cases, frames…)

It is a box containing fourteen books of different formats: cloth-bound books, newspapers, broadsheets and flip books.
